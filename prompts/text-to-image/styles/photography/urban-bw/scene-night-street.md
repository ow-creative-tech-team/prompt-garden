## Variations

**Less Rain:**
Remove "wet" and "rain-slicked", keep "damp pavement, recent rain"

**More Desolate:**
Add "abandoned street, no cars, broken street lights, urban decay"

**Busier Scene:**
Replace "empty sidewalks" with "few scattered pedestrians, distant figures"

**Time Variation:**
Change "night" to "late evening, blue hour" for twilight mood

## Technical Notes

- Reflections are key to this scene's visual interest
- Light sources should be specified: neon signs, street lamps, car headlights
- Weather effects (mist, rain) add depth and atmosphere
- Empty space creates tension and isolation
- Works best with wide-angle or medium focal lengths

## Lighting Setup

- **Primary**: Street lamps (warm/cool color in B&W = tonal variation)
- **Secondary**: Neon signs (bright specular highlights)
- **Ambient**: Night sky, minimal light pollution
- **Reflections**: Critical for composition, double the light sources

## Season/Weather Considerations

- **Best**: Fall/Winter for atmospheric conditions
- **Rain**: Essential for reflections, can specify "light rain" or "heavy downpour"
- **Fog/Mist**: Optional, adds depth and mystery
- **Temperature**: "Cold night air visible breath" adds realism
