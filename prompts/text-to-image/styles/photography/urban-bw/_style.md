## Prompt

Black and white photography, high contrast, dramatic shadows, street photography aesthetic, grainy film texture, sharp focus, deep blacks, bright highlights, gritty urban atmosphere, documentary style, Tri-X 400 film look, zone system exposure, f/8 depth of field, natural lighting

## Usage Examples

**Standalone:**
```
Black and white photography, high contrast, dramatic shadows, street photography aesthetic, grainy film texture, sharp focus, [subject description], urban environment
```

**Combined with subject:**
```
Black and white photography, high contrast, dramatic shadows + solitary figure in coat crossing empty street + gritty urban atmosphere, documentary style
```

**Combined with scene:**
```
[This style] + rainy night street, neon reflections on wet pavement, empty sidewalks + [subject if desired]
```

## Technical Parameters

- **Contrast**: High contrast, zone system approach
- **Grain**: Medium grain (Tri-X 400 equivalent)
- **Tonal Range**: Deep blacks to bright whites, full dynamic range
- **Focus**: Sharp, documentary-style clarity
- **Lighting**: Natural light preferred, street lights for night scenes

## Variations

**Softer Version:**
Replace "high contrast" with "medium contrast, softer tones"

**Grainier Version:**
Add "pushed film, increased grain, Tri-X 3200"

**Clean Modern:**
Replace "grainy film texture" with "digital clarity, minimal grain"

## Notes

- This style works particularly well with architectural elements and human subjects
- Strong geometric compositions enhance the dramatic effect
- Avoid overly busy scenes; negative space is your friend
- Morning and late afternoon light create the best shadow drama
- For best results, specify lighting direction when combining with scenes
