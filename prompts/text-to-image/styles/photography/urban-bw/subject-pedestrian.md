Solitary figure in dark overcoat, walking with purpose, slight forward lean, hands in pockets, anonymous silhouette, human scale against architecture, mid-stride, captured from behind or side angle, distance from camera, documentary candid moment

## Variations

**More Anonymous:**
"Silhouetted figure, face obscured, hat pulled low, collar up"

**More Visible:**
"Figure partially lit by street lamp, face visible in profile, thoughtful expression"

**Different Attire:**
- Winter: "Heavy coat, scarf, shoulders hunched against cold"
- Business: "Suit and briefcase, professional stride"
- Casual: "Casual jacket, relaxed posture, unhurried pace"

**Different Poses:**
- Stationary: "Standing still, looking up at buildings, contemplative"
- Motion: "Running, urgent movement, coat trailing"
- Interaction: "Lighting cigarette, checking phone, waiting at corner"

## Positioning & Framing

**Distance from camera:**
- Close: 10-15 feet (detail visible, more intimate)
- Medium: 20-30 feet (standard street photography)
- Far: 40+ feet (emphasizes environment, human scale)

**Angle:**
- Behind: Classic street photography, mysterious
- Side profile: Shows direction and purpose
- Three-quarter: Balance between anonymity and character
- Approaching: More confrontational, less common

**In frame:**
- Rule of thirds: Off-center for dynamic composition
- Leading lines: Use street lines to guide eye to figure
- Negative space: Isolation emphasized by empty space

## Character Details (Optional)

Add these for more specific character:
- Age: "elderly", "young adult", "middle-aged"
- Build: "tall and thin", "stocky", "average build"
- Gender: Can specify or leave ambiguous
- Accessories: "umbrella", "bag", "newspaper under arm"

## Motion & Gesture

- **Gait**: "confident stride", "hurried walk", "casual stroll"
- **Posture**: "upright", "slouched", "hunched against weather"
- **Hands**: "in pockets", "carrying coffee", "adjusting collar"
- **Direction**: "moving left to right", "approaching camera", "receding"

## Technical Photography Notes

- **Focus**: Figure sharp, background can have slight bokeh
- **Depth of field**: f/5.6 to f/11 for street photography sharpness
- **Shutter**: Fast enough to freeze motion (1/250s+) unless intentional blur
- **Moment**: Captured mid-stride for dynamic feeling
